Creating Simple Heatmaps

The U.S. Energy Information Administration has a great website and is a go-to resource for anything energy data-related. The site provides a great GUI tool to explore various datasets, however to really dig into historical data, it's easier to bring data in from the back. Luckily, EIA has developed an API to allow for easy access to historical data with Python.
